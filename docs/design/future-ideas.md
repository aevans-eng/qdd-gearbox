# Future Design Ideas

Notes on potential improvements for future revisions.

## Slimmer Bearing → Smaller Housing
- Switch bottom bearing to a thinner, wider variant
- Reduces total gearbox height
- **Must verify:** radial stress and fatigue via hand calcs
- **Constraint:** lower bearing cannot interfere with fastener threads

## Ring Gear as Housing ("Hamburger" Layout)
- Drop housing walls down to same diameter as ring gears
- Ring gears become the structural middle of the gearbox (like a hamburger patty)
- Eliminates the current approach of ring gear sitting *inside* the housing
- Combined with slimmer bearings, significantly reduces form factor

## Carrier Fastening & Planet Shaft Strategy
- **Option A: Shoulder bolts** — dual-purpose as carrier half constraints and planet bearing shafts
- **Option B: Pins + fasteners** — dedicated pins for planet bearing shafts, separate fasteners to connect carrier halves
- Worth exploring trade-offs (assembly complexity, tolerance stack, load path, part count)

## Integrated Motor + Controller Package
- Future iteration: combine gearbox with a 3D-printed motor housing
- Single package houses motor controller, brake resistor, and encoder
