## What We're Building
Create a quasi direct drive actuator by designing a gearbox to interface with a motor. To simplify and draw down the scope we'll adapt this to pre-made motor, encoder and ODrive board.

## In-Scope
- Mechanical design of a reduction stage (gearbox) optimized for FDM 3D printing
- Integration of a specific BLDC motor, magnetic encoder, and ODrive controller
- Interface design of the housing and output interface
- Basic modeling of our control system
- Compiling motor and controller capabilities and creating realistic output goals
- Designing a 3D printable gearbox and output interface

## Out-of-Scope
- Custom motor winding or controller PCB design (integrating, not inventing electronics)
- Complex firmware coding (basic ODrive config is assumed)
- Exotic manufacturing (no CNC aluminum; design must be printable)
