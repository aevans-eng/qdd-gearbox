CAD

CATIA V6 Parametric Architecture

~60-80 hours learning CATIA V6 from scratch. Skeleton-driven design -- every part reads from a single source of truth.

[ CATIA: full assembly cross-section showing gears, bearings, housing ]

Assembly Cross-Section

Skeleton-driven. Parts constrain to skeleton, never to each other.

Click for principles
  • Single source of truth -- all positioning in master skeleton
  • No B-Rep refs -- sketch on planes, reference wireframe only
  • Body-level patterning -- more stable than feature-level
  • Chain-of-zeros -- zero offsets at every hierarchy level
