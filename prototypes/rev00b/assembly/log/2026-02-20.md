### Engineering Decision: All Bearing Fits → Transition Fit

**What:** Changed all bearing interfaces from mixed press/transition fits to **transition fits only** (finger-press, matching coupon v1 main bearing feel).

**Why:**
- QDD backdrivability is the top priority — tighter fits = more friction
- FDM can't achieve true press fits reliably (±0.1-0.3mm variation)
- Herringbone gears self-center axially → minimal thrust loads on bearings
- FDM layer texture provides natural grip even without interference
- Prototype loads are low — bearing creep risk is minimal

**What changed:**
- `carrier_bearing_bore_offset`: 0.00mm → 0.10mm (carrier bore now matches lid bore)
- Planet bore offset stays +0.25mm (calibration-derived, targets same feel at smaller diameter)
- ISO designations updated in tolerance scheme for documentation

**Risk:** Bearing creep under load. Mitigation: FDM texture, low loads, loctite if needed.

**Details:** Full rationale in [`10-tolerance-scheme.md` — FDM Bearing Fit Philosophy](../10-tolerance-scheme.md).
