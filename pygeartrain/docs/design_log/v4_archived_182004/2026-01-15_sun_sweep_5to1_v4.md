## Design Insights

### Effect of b Parameter (0.3-0.45 range)
- **b=0.30:** Shorter teeth, more clearance, works with more planets
- **b=0.35:** Good balance of tooth height and clearance
- **b=0.40:** Slightly taller teeth, still valid
- **b=0.45:** Upper limit, may cause overlap in some configs

### Effect of Sun Teeth (S)
- **Smaller S (4-6):** More compact but fewer valid planet counts
- **Larger S (8-10):** Allows more planets, larger overall size
- **S=10 with N=5:** Best load distribution possible at 5:1 ratio

### Planet Count Trade-offs
| N | Load per Planet | Complexity | Clearance |
|---|-----------------|------------|-----------|
| 3 | 33% each | Simple | Most |
| 4 | 25% each | Moderate | Medium |
| 5 | 20% each | Complex | Least |
